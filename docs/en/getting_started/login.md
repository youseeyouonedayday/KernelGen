# Login

If you signed out and want sign in KernelGen, perform the following steps:

1. Access <https://KernelGen.flagos.io/login> in your browser.
2. Click **Start Building for Free**.
   ![alt text](../assets/images/start-building-from-free.png)
3. When the page is scrolled down to the bottom, click **Web Platform**.
   ![alt text](../assets/images/building-methods.png)
4. Sign in to KernelGen through phone number or Email address:

    {style=lower-alpha}
    1. In the **Login/Sign up** window, click the **Phone Login** tab or the **Email login** tab.
    2. Enter your phone number or Email address, and then click **Get Code**.
    3. Enter the received verification code for your phone number or Email address.
    4. Read and select the checkbox at the bottom to accept the **User Agreement** and **Privacy Policy** terms.

    ![alt text](../assets/images/2-login-and-signup-en.png)

5. Click **Login/Sign up**.

    The welcome page opens.
